Site Recommendations

Last modified February 26, 2026.

Astronomy

  • Daff Moon Phase is an Android app that I used to spot all five naked-eye planets (Mercury, Venus, Mars, Jupiter, and Saturn) in 2020.
  • Sunrise and Sunset Calculator not only calculates sunrise and sunset times, but also calculates civil twilight, nautical twilight, astronomical twilight, and solar noon times as well!

Natural Languages

  • Pleco is an amazing Chinese dictionary app that I recommend to anyone learning Mandarin.

Math

  • Roice Nelson, who developed the massive 120-cell puzzle, also wrote a paper with Henry Segerman on visualizing hyperbolic honeycombs!
  • 4D Euclidean Space dissects 4D shapes into layers so you can see how they’re constructed.

About Me

I currently work full-time on Visual Studio Code at Microsoft as a SWE.

Thanks to UWaterloo, I also have over two years of software development internship experience, from working on a GPU kernel at NVIDIA, to revamping a web application at Snowflake.

I like solving twisty puzzles such as the Rubik’s cube and Megaminx intuitively, though I used to be a speedcuber. In fact, my site is partially named after the ZZ method. I also solve higher-dimensional Rubik’s cubes.

Additionally, the environment matters to me. For example, I eat plant-based food, and purchase green power.

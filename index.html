About Me

I have been solving twisty puzzles such as the Rubikâ€™s cube and the Megaminx since grade school, and my site is partially named after the ZZ method, a 3x3x3 speedsolving method.

These days, I love hypercubing, a niche branch of cubing where one solves higher-dimensional twisty puzzles such as the five-dimensional 3x3x3x3x3. I also enjoy using commutators to figure out solutions to other unusual twisty puzzles such as the Rua or the 8-coloured {6,3,3}.

Some of my other interests include playing the recorder and contributing to open-source projects.
